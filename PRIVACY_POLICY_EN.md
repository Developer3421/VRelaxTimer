# VRelaxTimer — Privacy Policy (EN)

**Effective date:** 2025-12-29

This Privacy Policy explains what information VRelaxTimer stores on your device and how it is used.

## Summary
- VRelaxTimer is designed to work **offline**.
- The app does **not** require an account.
- The app does **not** collect or transmit personal data.
- The app stores only:
  1) your **selected interface language**, and
  2) whether the **User Agreement** was **accepted**.

## What data is stored
VRelaxTimer stores the following small settings locally on your device:
- **Interface language** (e.g., English, Ukrainian, German, etc.)
- **User Agreement status** (accepted or not accepted)

These settings are used only to:
- remember your chosen UI language, and
- determine whether the User Agreement should be shown again on startup.

## What data is NOT collected
VRelaxTimer does not:
- collect analytics or telemetry,
- collect location data,
- collect device identifiers for tracking,
- collect contacts, files, or browsing history,
- send your messages to an online server.

## Data sharing
VRelaxTimer does not share or sell any user data. The app does not transmit the stored settings to any third party.

## Data retention
The stored settings remain on your device until you uninstall the app or delete the app’s local settings.

## Changes to this policy
If this policy changes, the updated version will be provided with the app (for example, in an updated file or store listing).
